Frequently asked questions

How do I capture a new question?

Tap the capture button on the main screen, then type your question or use the microphone for voice dictation. Transcription happens entirely on your device. The question starts in the Incomplete state — update it to Ongoing or Answered from the question's detail page as your research progresses.

How does tagging work?

Every question can carry tags in four dimensions: Specialty, Anatomy, Disease, and Kind. Open a question, expand the Tags panel, and tap Add Tag. You can search existing tags, create new ones by typing a name, and rename or delete any tag by long-pressing it in the picker (right-click on Mac). Renaming a tag updates every question that uses it.

How do I add sources and citations?

In a question's Sources section, add a DOI or URL. For DOIs, bibliographic metadata (title, authors, journal) is fetched automatically from CrossRef. While writing your synthesis note, tap the cite button next to any source to insert a numbered citation pill like [1] at the cursor. Citations renumber automatically, and tapping a pill jumps to its source.

What is the Case Repository?

The repository collects your favorite images across all cases. Star an image on any question and it appears in the Case Repository, automatically inheriting the question's tags so you can filter by specialty. To remove an image from the repository, tap its star — either on the question or directly on the repository tile. Removing it never deletes the image; it stays on its original question.

How do I export a question to PDF?

Open a question and use the export button in the toolbar. The PDF includes the question, tags, sources, synthesis note with citations, and case images — ready to share or archive.

My data isn't syncing between devices.
  • Make sure both devices are signed in to the same Apple Account.
  • Check Settings > Apple Account > iCloud and confirm iCloud Drive and Medical Casebook are enabled.
  • Confirm both devices have a network connection — iCloud sync pauses offline and catches up automatically.
  • Low iCloud storage can stall sync; check your available space.
  • If it still won't sync, restart both devices, then email us.
Is my data backed up? What if I delete the app?

With iCloud sync enabled, your content lives in your private iCloud database and is restored automatically when you reinstall the app on any device signed in to the same Apple Account. Without iCloud, your data exists only on-device and is included in normal device backups — deleting the app deletes its local data, so we recommend keeping iCloud sync on.

Voice capture isn't transcribing.

Voice capture needs microphone and speech-recognition permission. Check Settings > Privacy & Security > Microphone and > Speech Recognition and make sure Medical Casebook is allowed. On-device dictation also requires the dictation language to be downloaded (Settings > General > Keyboard > Dictation).

Does the app collect any of my data?

No. There are no servers, accounts, analytics, or tracking. Everything stays on your device and in your own private iCloud. See the privacy policy for details.
